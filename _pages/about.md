Hi! I am a predoctoral researcher at Toulouse School of Economics in the Econometrics and Empirical Economics Department. I like intertwining Economics and Public Policy to shed light on real-world problems, using both reduced forms and structural models. My research interests lie in Economics of Education, Development Economics, Political Economy and Culture, with insights from behavioural science.
